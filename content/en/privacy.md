# Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: [TBD upon launch]*

## Overview

Justice Access Foundation ("JAF," "we," "our," or "us") is a California nonprofit public benefit corporation and 501(c)(3) public charity. This Privacy Policy describes how JAF collects, uses, retains, and protects information submitted through this website (justiceaccessfoundation.org).

JAF does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

---

## Information We Collect

**Information You Provide Voluntarily**

When you submit a contact form, donation inquiry form, or other inquiry through this website, we collect:

- Your name
- Your email address
- Any message content, notes, or designations you include

We do not collect payment information through this website. Donation inquiries submitted through this site are followed up with separate, secure payment instructions.

**Automatically Collected Information**

Our website hosting provider (Netlify) may collect standard server log information, including IP addresses, browser type, and pages accessed, for security and operational purposes. This information is not used to identify individual visitors.

We do not use tracking pixels, third-party advertising networks, or behavioral analytics tools.

---

## How We Use Information

Information submitted through this website is used solely to:

- Respond to your inquiry
- Process and acknowledge charitable contributions
- Provide written acknowledgment of donations as required by IRC Section 170(f)(8)
- Maintain organizational records as required by applicable law

---

## Retention

We retain contact and donation inquiry records for a period consistent with IRS recordkeeping requirements for public charities (generally seven years for financial records). Records are maintained securely and are accessible only to authorized JAF personnel.

---

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

- **Right to Know**: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
- **Right to Delete**: You may request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to applicable exceptions (including records retention obligations).
- **Right to Correct**: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information.
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise these rights, contact JAF at the address below. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

JAF does not sell personal information and does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. Accordingly, no opt-out mechanism for sale or sharing of personal information is required.

## Security

JAF implements reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information submitted through this website, including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. No transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.
